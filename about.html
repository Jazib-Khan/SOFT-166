Welcome to my Tic Tac Toe game

Play against your friends

Tic tac toe is a 2 player turn game that takes only minutes to learn how to play...

If you feel comfortable with the rules then click Play

How to play:

Step 1: Grab a friend

Step 2: Draw a 3 by 3 grid like the one demonstrated to the right

Step 3: Understand that the aim of the game is to get 3 in a row of your letter which is how the game is won. This is also demonstrated in the image on the right as player X has alligned itself 3 times in the diagonal direction

Step 4: Decide on which player goes first and whoever that is draws either an X or O on any of the squares in the grid they desire

Step 5: Then player 2 draws on the grid with the opposite letter to player 1

Step 6: Alternate moves from player 1 and 2 until one of the players has drawn three in a row and they're the winner or until no one wins in which all the squares have been used up. In this case go back to step 2 but swap which player goes first.
